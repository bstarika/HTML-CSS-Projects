Sunday Funday in Estes Park

Every Sunday, our group takes a trip to the mountains. Here are some neat facts on our latest adventure in Estes!

▴Located ~ 90 miles northwest of Denver at 7,522 ft. above sea level.

▴Sits in the heart of the Rocky Mountains.

▴Home to 300 miles of hiking trails.

▴Outdoor adventures include hiking, snowshoeing, rock climbing and so much more!
